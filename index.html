B.C.’s 100 top paid public employees

Source: Postmedia’s public sector salary database, B.C. government

  • Each of the 100 highest-paid public sector employees in 2020-21 earned over $390,000.Scroll for more...

  • Only 28 were women

  • UBC, the Provincial Health Services Authority (PHSA), and Powerex corporation were the only organizations with three or more staff in the top 100. Thirty-six of them worked for UBC.

    Only six UBC staff were women

  • There were 18 people who earned over a half a million dollars in 2020-21.

    Only four were women

  • B.C. is one of only three provinces without provincial pay equity legislation, which requires employers to give equal pay to women and men for equal work. A recent study from Statistics Canada found that in 2021B.C. had the second largest gender wage gap in the country after Alberta, with women earning 86 cents for every dollar paid to a man.
