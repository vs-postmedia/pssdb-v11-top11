B.C.’s 100 top-paid public employees

Source: Postmedia’s public sector salary database, B.C. government

  • Each of the 100 highest-paid public sector employees in 2020-21 earned over $390,000.Scroll for more...

  • Only 28 were women

  • Three organizations employed 83 of B.C.’s 100 highest earners: UBC, Powerex and the Provincial Health Services Agency.

    Only 24 per cent were women

  • There were 18 people who earned over a half a million dollars in 2020-21.

    Only four were women

  • Women in the top 100 were most likely to reach top-paid positions at health authorities, a sector where women are overrepresented. Roughly 73 per cent of B.C. health-care workers earning $75,000 or more are women, according to Postmedia’s data.

    60 of B.C.’s highest-paid women worked in health care
